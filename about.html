About Us

Potomac Way principals are passionate about building businesses through their distinct and critical phases of development. We seek to impart our diverse knowledge to you through the delivery of our advisory services. Our team has been carefully selected to represent a wide range of functional and industry skills. We have all previously worked together, sharing a core set of values and focus on results.

OUR FOCUS

Our business is about serving your business, driven to achieve results for you. We have an extensive array of tools, templates and methodologies to draw on, uniquely applying them to your situation. We work alongside existing team members to augment and compliment their efforts, while delivering a level of results beyond what is available from current resources.

Principals include executives with experience in Financial Services (Banking, Electronic Payments) Telecommunications, Information Technology and Government. Potomac Way consultants are multi-disciplinary and have specialities in strategic planning, mergers and acquisitions, sales and marketing, finance and accounting, product development, project management, process improvement, restructuring, credit and risk management and many others.

WHO WE ARE

John S. Cramp

A proven business executive with over 25 years of leadership experience in Fortune 500, start-up and growth companies.
